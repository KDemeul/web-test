In a team of 5, we designed a robot that could find its way into a house (the maze), detect, localize and identify the different objects in the house.

During phase 1, the robot should explore the whole house and build a map of its environment (including object positions and identities).

During phase 2, it is supposed to fetch the previously identified objects.

Our solution relies on four different parts:

  • Motion controller
  • Simultaneous Localization and Mapping (SLAM) algorithm
  • Local trajectory controller and global path finder
  • Detection and identification of objects "on the go"
